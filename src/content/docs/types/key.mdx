<LSLType name="key"/>

A 128 bit unique identifier (UUID).

The key is represented as hexidecimal characters (A-F and 0-9), grouped into sections (8,4,4,4,12 characters) and separated by hyphens (for a total of 36 characters). e.g. &quot;A822FF2B-FF02-461D-B45D-DCD10A2DE0C2&quot;.
